My Childhood: Hobbies & Toys

A friendly snapshot of things I loved as a child.

Dancing

I used to love dancing in front of the mirror to my favourite songs. A few times, I was the lead dancer at my studio and I felt like a star!

Reading

Reading fairy tales was my favourite way to spend a quiet afternoon. I still remember getting lost in the magical worlds of those storybooks.

Toys

Dolls

I had a collection of dolls that I loved to dress up and style their hair. My favourite doll had a pink dress and went everywhere with me.

Tea Sets

I used to host pretend tea parties with my miniature tea set and invite all my toys. It was so much fun pouring invisible tea and pretending to be the hostess.
